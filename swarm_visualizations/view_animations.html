üîí Respect Boundary Feature

Safety-First Design: Automatically enforced when a target position is specified.

  • Automatic: 10% of search space diagonal by default
  • Mandatory: Cannot be disabled for safety-critical applications
  • Applications: Satellites, drones, robots, collision avoidance
  • Smart: Particles converge to sphere around target, not on it

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Notice: When you provide a target_position, the respect boundary is automatically enabled and cannot be disabled. This prevents particles from getting too close to potentially hazardous targets.

Usage:
swarm = Swarm(..., target_position=[x, y, z])
# Respect boundary automatically enforced!
